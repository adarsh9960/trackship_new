Franchise FAQs

Home Franchise FAQs

What is the minimum investment for a Trackship franchise?

Our retail courier point model starts at just ₹19,999. Logistics Hubs and Regional Master models require higher investment based on city tier and capacity.

How long does the onboarding process take?

Typically, you can go live within 7-14 days. This includes portal setup, documentation, and the initial training phase.

Do I need previous experience in logistics?

While experience is a plus, it is not mandatory. Our comprehensive training and dedicated support managers guide you through every operational detail.

What kind of space do I need?

For a Courier Point, a shop or office space of 150-200 sq ft is sufficient. Logistics Hubs require at least 1,000 sq ft of warehouse-capable space.
